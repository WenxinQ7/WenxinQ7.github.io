Yuan was born in Nantong, Jiangsu, a small but nice city in east coast of China. She spent her undergraduate years in Fudan University in Shanghai, where she got her Bachelor's degree in chemistry. After that, she earned her Ph.D. degree in Chemistry in Michigan State University under the direction of Prof. Thomas Hamann. During her research she realized that scientists spend too much time on repeated work, which is a waste of resource. She decided to explore the area of computer science to realize the automation of processes in scientific research as well as in daily life. Now she is studying computer science at Northeastern University in Seattle.

Yuan enjoys playing sports, especially badminton, archery and skiing. She also plays video games like CSGO and PUBG (PUBG is a better game than Fortnite yay!). She likes various genres of music from melodic hardcore to indie.
